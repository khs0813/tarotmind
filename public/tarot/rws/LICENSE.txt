Relevant notes from the source pages checked on 2026-07-05:
- Wikimedia Commons marks these files as Public Domain.
- The original Rider-Waite Tarot was first published in 1909/1910.
- The Commons pages state the work is in the public domain in the United States because it was published before January 1, 1931.
- The Commons pages identify Pamela Colman Smith as the artist and note Arthur Edward Waite as copyright holder.

Operational rule:
Do not replace or add tarot card images in this folder unless the replacement is also confirmed as Public Domain, CC0, CC BY, commercially usable, or covered by a paid license.
